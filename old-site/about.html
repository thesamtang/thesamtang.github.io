ABOUT ME

"Indifference towards people and the reality in which they live is actually the one and only cardinal sin in design"
-Dieter Rams

I am an architectural studies and computer science double major at Amherst College and my life mission is to find and maintain a meaningful, scalable harmony between function and aesthetics. The principles of design and visual thinking affect everything I involve myself in and my work is primarily driven by the pursuit of memorable user/viewer experiences.

Currently, I am slaving away on my architecture thesis and working as an IT specialist for Amherst College. I have previously worked as an SDE Intern at Amazon, web assistant at Amherst College, and design intern at Sagemax Bioceramics. My photography has been featured in Advanced Photoshop Magazine, and I am a nationally-recognized competitive public speaker.

I grew up in the Seattle area and had the privilege of experiencing, year after year, the best summer weather that planet Earth has to offer.
